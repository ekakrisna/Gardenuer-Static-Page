Our Mission

At Gardeneur, our passion lies in supporting and bringing together diverse talents of Bay Area local makers through our Marketplace events and HubOn transport. Our Marketplace events serve as an opportunity for eco-entrepreneurs, plant growers, specialty food makers, green artisans, and more to showcase their creations, while HubOn helps them fulfill online pre-orders through our 50+ pickup locations in the Bay Area.

With a flourishing community of over 200+ makers, we believe in fostering sustainability by facilitating local transactions. We invite you to join us at our next event. Let's build connections, celebrate creativity, and grow together!

What is HubOn?

With HubOn, local makers can now make their online pre-orders available for pickup at any of our 50+ store partners. Many local makers use HubOn to transport delicate goods from birthday cakes to pet foods to plants. Our goal is to enable them to instantly serve the whole Bay Area without opening physical stores.

Unlike online marketplaces, HubOn only charges a $6.99 transportation fee and does not charge hefty commissions. We aggregate our deliveries through our hub partners reducing CO2 emission by 10X compared to door-to-door delivery services. We also hire and train W2 employees to ensure that every order is hand delivered with the utmost care and attention.

Take a step towards sustainability and try HubOn transport for FREE today!
